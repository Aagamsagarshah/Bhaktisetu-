About Siddhivinayak

The Shree Siddhivinayak Ganapati Mandir is one of the most visited temples in India, dedicated to Lord Ganesha. Located in Prabhadevi, Mumbai, it is known as the fulfiller of wishes and attracts millions of devotees including Bollywood stars and politicians.

History & Significance

Built in 1801, the temple was initially a small structure. Over the years, it expanded into a grand complex with a gold-plated inner sanctum. Lord Ganesha’s idol is unique — with the trunk tilted to the right, symbolizing rare Siddhi.
