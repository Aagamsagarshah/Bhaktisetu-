Famous Pilgrimage Temples

Somnath Temple

Located in Gujarat, one of the twelve Jyotirlinga shrines of Shiva.

Dwarka Temple

The ancient city of Lord Krishna, Gujarat, famous for its Krishna Temple.

Rameshwaram Temple

Located in Tamil Nadu, famous for Ramanathaswamy Temple and pilgrimage circuit.

Palitana Temple

A major Jain pilgrimage center in Gujarat with hundreds of temples.

Sidhivinayak Temple

Famous Ganesh temple located in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Ambaji Temple

Located in Gujarat, dedicated to Goddess Amba, important Shakti Peeth.

Temple Details

Spiritual Significance

Each temple holds historical and religious importance. Pilgrims visit to seek blessings, perform rituals, and experience devotion.

Visiting Tips

Check timings, dress appropriately, respect local customs, and plan your visit to avoid peak hours for a serene experience.

Travel & Accommodation

Most temples have nearby accommodation and travel facilities. Book in advance, especially during festivals.
